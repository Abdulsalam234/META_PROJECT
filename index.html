Welcome to Lucky Shrub Farms


Not your typical farm and not your typical website, the Lucky Shrub Farms offers a safe haven to farm animals and plants across the country. Its website, which is filled with sweet pictures of its furry and feathered residents, also uses lighthearted animal illustrations throughout its pages. Filled with great action buttons and tons of content, the website invites you to really experience the farm virtually, making you feel like you are a part of their animal-loving community, even if you never have the opportunity to actually visit them in New York.
